About e-waste 
E-waste includes items such as computers, laptops, mobile phones, televisions, printers, batteries, chargers, and circuit boards. These devices contain both valuable materials (like copper, gold, and silver) and hazardous substances (such as lead, mercury, and cadmium)
Improper disposal of e-waste can cause serious environmental and health problems. Toxic chemicals can pollute soil, water, and air, affecting humans, animals, and plants. Therefore, proper management, recycling, and safe disposal of e-waste are essential.
Importance of E-waste Management
Reduces environmental pollution
Prevents health hazards
Saves natural resources
Supports recycling and reuse
Promotes sustainable development.
